Privacy Policy & SMS Terms

Last Updated: April 2026

Paris Appliance Repair LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with our website, request service, or opt-in to receive SMS (text) messages from us.

1. Information We Collect

When you fill out our online contact form or call us to schedule an appliance repair, we collect the following basic information necessary to provide our services:

  • Your Name
  • Phone Number
  • Service Address (collected over the phone or via email)
  • Information regarding your appliance and the repair issue

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide strictly for internal business operations, including:

  • Scheduling, confirming, and managing your appliance repair appointments.
  • Communicating with you regarding parts ordering, technician arrival times, and estimates.
  • Sending invoices and requesting feedback on our service.

3. Strict 10DLC SMS & Data Sharing Policy

We value your privacy. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information, phone number, or SMS opt-in data with any third parties, affiliates, or partners for marketing or promotional purposes under any circumstances. All data sharing mentioned in this policy strictly excludes mobile opt-in data and consent; your SMS consent is never shared with anyone.

4. SMS Messaging Terms & Opt-Out

By providing your phone number and requesting service, you consent to receive necessary SMS text messages from Paris Appliance Repair LLC regarding your appointments, technician updates, and service follow-ups. Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier. Message frequency varies based on your repair needs.

  • How to Opt-Out: You may opt-out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message we send you. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message, and no further text messages will be sent.
  • How to Get Help: If you need assistance with our text messaging, reply HELP to any of our messages or call us directly at (903) 272-0840.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

We may use trusted third-party tools (such as Formspree for website contact forms or secure payment processors) solely to facilitate our business operations. These providers act as data processors and are bound by strict confidentiality agreements. They are not permitted to use your data for their own marketing purposes.
